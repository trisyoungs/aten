# Creates a condensed water system
#

# Draw molecule, then load and associate forcefield
newmodel water
addatom H
move 1.0 0.0 0.0
addchain O
roty 70.5
move 1.0 0.0 0.0 
addchain H
printmodel

loadff wff data/ff/water.ff
ffmodel

createexpression
modelenergy
printenergy

# Create the empty cell for our water to go in to.
# For cubic cells, nwater should be approximately :     10 A  = 33
#							20 A = 267
newmodel box
setcell 10.0 10.0 10.0 90.0 90.0 90.0
ffmodel
#elec ewaldauto 5.0e-6
printexpression

# Request the addition of water molecules. If we wish to cram in more (to get a higher density) decrease vdwscale.
addcomponent bulk water 33
printcomponents
vdwscale 1.0

disorder 10
